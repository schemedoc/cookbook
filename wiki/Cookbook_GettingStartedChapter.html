Getting Started With PLT Scheme

Introduction

PLT Scheme differs from `standard' Scheme in a number of ways. The differences are mostly either minor cosmetic issues or major enhancements to the functionality of Scheme. This section gives you a rundown of the differences and extensions you're likely to come across if you have some familiarity with other Scheme systems, and tells you how to get started using PLT Scheme.
