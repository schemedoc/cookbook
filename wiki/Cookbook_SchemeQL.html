SchemeQL

Location

http://schematics.sourceforge.net/

Description

SchemeQL is a s-expression based language embedded in Scheme that expresses SQL queries. As a SchemeQL queries are first-class objects queries can be combined and composed in ways not possible with text-based encodings of SQL.
