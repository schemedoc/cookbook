Modules

A very nice introduction to modules in PLT Scheme is How To Use Modules from How to Use Scheme.

The more theoretically minded will want to read Matthew Flatt's paper "Composable and Compilable GettingStartedMacros: You Want it When?" from ICFP 2002 (the International Conference on Functional Programming).

It goes without saying that what the HelpDesk has to say on modules is also worth a read.
