Becoming An Author

To become an author. visit TWikiRegistration and fill out the registration form. Anyone can become a cookbook author, and contribute to the cookbook. Once you have registered with the site you can modify and add recipes as you see fit. Please note that all contributions are covered by the Copyright statement in BookIntroduction.
