Author Guide

Introduction

This chapter covers the practices and conventions of contributing to the Cookbook. For ease of editing it is laid out like any other chapter, with recipes and so forth. If you edit any recipes please be careful with the TopicOrder, as the order of recipes is quite important for this chapter.

The Structure of the Cookbook

The Cookbook consists of Chapters, which contains Sections, which in turn contain Recipes. Recipes are the basic unit of content in the cookbook. Each recipe should usually have its own TWiki topic (page). In some cases, it may make sense to use a single topic for a series of short, related recipes.
