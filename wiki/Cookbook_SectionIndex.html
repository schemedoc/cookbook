This page is a prototype, under development.

It is like the new auto-generated TOC, but with chapters having their own headings, rather than being bulleted items.

Currently, only the Numbers and String Recipes chapters are shown completely correctly, because those chapters have been structured using the new approach proposed in CookbookStructure. Other chapters show an incomplete and possibly empty list of sections.

This page supports an alternate view which includes individual recipes, in addition to sections. Again, this only works for Numbers and String Recipes at present. If you don't see a list of recipes in the Recipes section for String Recipes, then click here and look again. [Note: clicking on a recipe in this view currently doesn't take you directly to the selected recipe; only to the chapter. That's a bug which will hopefully be fixed.]
