RnRS

RnRs ? is the name is the family name of the programming language standards for Scheme. The R2RS is "The Revised Revised Report on the Algorithmic Language Scheme", the R3RS is "The Revised Revised Revised Report on the Algorithmic Language Scheme", and soforth.

The current standard is R5RS.
