PLT Scheme

PLT Scheme is the Scheme dialect on which this cookbook is focused.

PLT Scheme consists of a family of software, including the DrScheme graphical Scheme environment, and the MzScheme console-based Scheme implementation.

The DrScheme environment includes the Help Desk which provides extensive information about PLT Scheme, as well as Scheme in general. All of the PLT manuals are accessible via the Help Desk. Bug reports are submitted through the Help Desk.

For more information about PLT Scheme, see the plt-scheme.org Web site in addition to this Cookbook.
